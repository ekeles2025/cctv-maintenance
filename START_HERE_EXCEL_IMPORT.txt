## How to Use

### Step 1: Login as Admin
Sign in to your account

### Step 2: Go to Import Page
Click "الأعطال" → "استيراد من Excel"

### Step 3: Download Template
Click the template button

### Step 4: Add Your Data
Fill in the columns:
- Camera Name
- Fault Type
- Description
- Reported By
- Technician (optional)

### Step 5: Upload & Import
Select file, click "استيراد الأعطال"
